Chainmate is the first fully on-chain chess platform that combines the timeless strategy of chess with blockchain technology, creating a transparent, rewarding, and globally accessible gaming experience on Mantle Network.

##  The Opportunity

Chess is one of the world's most popular games with over **600 million players globally**. The online chess market is valued at **$2.1 billion** and growing. Chainmate brings this massive audience to Web3, offering:

- **Transparent Gameplay**: Every move is recorded on-chain, eliminating disputes and ensuring fair play
- **Real Rewards**: Players earn tokens and NFTs through competitive play and achievements
- **Global Accessibility**: Low transaction fees on Mantle Network make competitive chess accessible worldwide
- **Proven Demand**: Chess.com has 100M+ registered users; we're bringing them to Web3

## üéÆ Game Experience

### **Player vs Player (PvP) Mode**
- **Competitive Matches**: Challenge players worldwide in ranked matches
- **Staking System**: Players stake tokens to enter matches, winner takes all
- **Leaderboards**: Climb the ranks and compete for top positions
- **Tournaments**: Participate in organized tournaments with prize pools

### **Player vs Computer (PvC) Mode**
- **AI Opponents**: Practice against on-chain AI with varying difficulty levels
- **Free Play**: Learn and improve without financial risk
- **Achievement System**: Unlock rewards by defeating AI at different levels
- **Training Mode**: Perfect your strategy before competitive play

### **Social & Community Features**
- **Match History**: View all your games on-chain, permanently recorded
- **Player Profiles**: Showcase your chess rating, wins, and achievements
- **Spectator Mode**: Watch live matches and learn from top players
- **Community Challenges**: Participate in community-wide events and quests
